Rubric - Abbasid Revolution Historiography Essay

An A-range essay for this assignment does all of the following:
- Presents a specific, arguable thesis in the introduction
- Uses at least five concrete historical details and integrates them into an argument
- Explains both short-term causes and longer-term consequences
- Distinguishes between social, political, and dynastic explanations instead of collapsing them together
- Includes at least one counterargument and answers it
- Uses the supplied source packet with attribution that names the source or speaker
- Integrates at least one short quoted phrase with context and explanation
- Compares a primary source perspective with a later analytical or historiographical interpretation
- Keeps paragraphs focused and analytical rather than descriptive
- Avoids dropped quotations, vague references, and generic filler

Essays below this level usually become too general, do not interpret the evidence, or fail to explain why the evidence changes the reader's understanding of the Abbasid Revolution.
