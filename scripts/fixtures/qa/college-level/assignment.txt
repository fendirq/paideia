Assignment Prompt - Abbasid Revolution Historiography Essay

Write a 1200 to 1400 word essay that answers the following question:

To what extent should the Abbasid Revolution be understood as a social revolution rather than simply a dynastic transfer of power?

Your essay must distinguish between the immediate causes of Abbasid victory and the longer-term transformations that followed between 744 and 833. Build an argument that weighs competing explanations rather than listing them.

Required elements:
- A clear interpretive thesis in the first paragraph
- At least five specific pieces of historical evidence
- Discussion of Abu Muslim and Khurasan
- Discussion of the mawali question
- Discussion of the Battle of the Zab
- Discussion of Baghdad or early Abbasid administrative change
- At least one counterargument that you take seriously before answering it
- At least one short quotation from the supplied source packet
- Explicit comparison between a primary source perspective and a later analytical interpretation

Do not use generic phrases such as "the source shows" unless you name the source or speaker. Do not just summarize events. Explain why the evidence matters for your interpretation.
