Assignment Prompt - Abbasid Revolution Interpretive Essay

Write a 1200 to 1400 word essay answering the following question:

To what extent should the Abbasid Revolution be understood as a social revolution rather than a dynastic transfer of power?

Your essay must distinguish between the immediate causes of Abbasid victory and the longer-term transformations that followed between 744 and 833. Do not simply summarize events. Build an interpretation that weighs competing explanations.

Required elements:
- A clear thesis in the opening paragraph
- At least five specific pieces of historical evidence
- Discussion of Abu Muslim and Khurasan
- Discussion of the mawali question
- Discussion of the Battle of the Zab
- Discussion of Baghdad or early Abbasid administrative change
- At least one counterargument that you answer directly
- At least one short quotation from the source packet
- Explicit comparison between a primary source perspective and a later analytical interpretation

Write in direct analytical prose. Do not pad with generic transitions or filler. Every body paragraph should make a claim, use evidence, and explain what that evidence changes in the argument.
