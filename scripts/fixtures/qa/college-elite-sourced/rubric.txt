Rubric - Abbasid Revolution Interpretive Essay

An A-range essay for this assignment does all of the following:
- Presents a clear, arguable thesis in the first paragraph
- Distinguishes short-term causes from longer-term consequences
- Uses at least five concrete historical details
- Integrates at least one short quotation smoothly into the sentence flow
- Names the source or speaker when using quotations
- Compares a primary source perspective with a later analytical interpretation
- Uses body paragraphs that follow a claim -> evidence -> explanation structure
- Addresses a counterargument directly rather than just mentioning it
- Avoids descriptive padding, dropped quotations, and vague references to unnamed sources
- Maintains clear academic prose without sounding like a model answer or a list of rubric items

Essays below this level usually stay too general, rely on packet-style source references, or fail to explain why the evidence changes the interpretation.
